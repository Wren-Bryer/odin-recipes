Hot Tamale Pie

Description

This is a Hot Tamale Pie recipe.

Ingedients

  • Diced Onion
  • 3 lbs ground Beef
  • 2 small cans tomato paste
  • 4 cans water
  • 3 cans corn
  • salt
  • pepper
  • chili powder
  • Instant cornbread mix

Steps

  1. Saute onions in a cast iron skillet until fragrant and translucent
  2. Add ground beef to the pan
  3. Once the ground beef is cooked thouroughly, add tomato paste and water. Mix well.
  4. Add corn. Mix well.
  5. Simmer until thickened
  6. Prepare cornbreak mix
  7. Pour batter ontop, and place in oven at 400 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until cornbread is cooked
