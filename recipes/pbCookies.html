Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

This is a peanut butter cookie recipe.

Ingedients

  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix all ingredients
  3. Roll dough into balls
  4. place on an ungreased baking sheet; flatten with fork
  5. Bake until crisp, 12-15 minutes
